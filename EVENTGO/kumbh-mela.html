Kumbh Mela 2025

Kumbh Mela is the largest spiritual gathering in the world, held every 12 years in India. Millions of pilgrims gather at sacred rivers to take a holy dip, believed to cleanse sins and bring spiritual liberation. The festival features religious ceremonies, cultural performances, and a vibrant display of devotion.
