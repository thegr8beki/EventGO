Day of the Dead 2025

The Day of the Dead (Día de los Muertos) is a Mexican tradition that honors deceased loved ones. Families create colorful altars (ofrendas) adorned with marigolds, candles, and photos. The festival is a joyful celebration of life and memory, featuring parades, music, and traditional foods like sugar skulls and pan de muerto.
